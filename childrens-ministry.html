Children's Ministry

We currently offer children's ministry ages 0 - 12 years old where we teach how to worship, share their faith and grow in their knowledge of the Bible. We want to empower children to walk in their callings and equip families to deepen their walks with the Lord.

Our classrooms teach children at their age levels how to experience God, read the word, share their faith and walk in the fullness of salvation, which includes all the gifts of the Holy Spirit.
